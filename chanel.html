Coco Chanel

Chanel meets Coco: An Origin Story

Coco Chanel is widely regarded as one of the most influential fashion designers of the 20th century. She revolutionized women's fashion by introducing simple, elegant, and comfortable styles that liberated women from the constraints of corsets, frills, and feathers. She also popularized the use of fabrics such as jersey, tweed, and wool, and introduced iconic items such as the little black dress, the Chanel suit, and the Chanel No. 5 perfume.
